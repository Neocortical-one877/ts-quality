# Mission

`ts-quality` exists to turn TypeScript/JavaScript change evidence into deterministic, inspectable decisions about trust, risk, and required follow-up work.

In practice, that means:
- translating coverage, mutation, invariant, governance, and legitimacy evidence into stable artifacts
- making missing or weak evidence explicit instead of hiding it behind headline confidence language
- giving operators a review surface that explains *why* a change is trusted, blocked, or still under-evidenced
